About Hopster

An easy-to-use app that solves the problem of high rising gas prices.

Since the war in Ukraine began, the prices for gas & electricity are getting more expensive. Hopster enables you to exactly track the costs of car rides by factoring in local gas & electricity prices, the driven distance, as well as, car specific parameters. Furthermore, the app collects important user data (commonly driven routes, commonly used cars, ...) which may be used for future case studies.
